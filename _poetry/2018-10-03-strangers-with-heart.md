title: "Strangers with Heart"
date: 2018-10-03
categories: Poetry
---

What are we, if we are not lovers?
Strangers with a heart
that are no longer each others?

I said to you once
that we couldn't be friends.
Not when something as special
as what we had ends.

So does that make us
lovers that never speak?
Each in love with a lover
we could not keep.

Are we lovers who now no longer love?
Or lovers who love each other, just not enough.
